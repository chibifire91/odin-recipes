Sticky Finger Chicken

Description

A Winger's copycat recipe, Sticky Finger Chicken is a delightfully sweet and spicy breaded chicken tender treat!

Ingredients

  • chicken tenders
  • pank bread crumbs
  • eggs
  • frank's hot sauce
  • brown sugar
  • water
  • garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Pat dry the chicken tenders.
  3. Whisk eggs together in a bowl with 1 tsp water and dip chicken tenders into egg wash.
  4. Dip eggy tenders into bowl of panko bread crumbs.
  5. Place tenders onto oil sprayed baking sheet.
  6. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or until chicken tenders reach a temperature of 165 degrees.
  7. While tenders cook, melt butter in saucepan.
  8. Add brown sugar and hot sauce, stir together, and bring to a boil.
  9. Once boiling, remove sauce from heat and add water and garlic powder.
  10. When breaded tenders are finished cooking, coat them in the sauce.
  11. Serve with ranch or blue cheese dressing and enjoy!
