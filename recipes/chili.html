Chili

Description

A simple cold weather comfort food whose ingredients are measured not with cups and spoons but with heart and soul. Made with ground meat, tomatoes, beans, and an assortment of spices.

Ingredients

  • ground meat (I use turkey)
  • onion
  • dark red kidney beans
  • pinto beans
  • black beans
  • diced tomatoes
  • beef broth
  • tomato sauce
  • chili powder
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • paprika
  • minced garlic
  • brown sugar
  • powdered ghost pepper (if an extra kick is desired)
  • sour cream
  • shredded cheese
  • hot sauce (I prefer Louisiana)
  • green onions

Steps

  1. Brown your ground meat in a large, deep skillet.
  2. Add your onions and cook until translucent.
  3. Add minced garlic.
  4. Rinse your black beans before adding to the skillet.
  5. Add your pinto and kidney beans.
  6. Add your diced tomatoes and tomato sauce.
  7. Add beef broth.
  8. Add brown sugar.
  9. Add spices and stir everything together.
  10. Bring to a boil, then turn to low and let simmer until ready.
  11. As soon as your tummy can't stand the wait any longer, serve with whatever toppings you desire and enjoy.
