Chili

Description

A simple cold weather comfort food whose ingredients are measured not with cups and spoons but with heart and soul. Made with ground meat, tomatoes, beans, and an assortment of spices.

Ingredients

  • ground meat (I use turkey)
  • onion
  • dark red kidney beans
  • pinto beans
  • black beans
  • diced tomatoes
  • beef broth
  • tomato sauce
  • chili powder
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • paprika
  • minced garlic
  • brown sugar
  • powdered ghost pepper (if an extra kick is desired)
  • sour cream
  • shredded cheese
  • hot sauce (I prefer Louisiana)
  • green onions
