Sushibowl

Description

Deconstructed sushi.

Quick, easy, delicious.

Ingredients

  • cooked sushi rice
  • imitation crab meat
  • avocado
  • cucumber
  • nori
  • spicy mayo
  • sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Cook rice according to directions on package.
  2. Let rice cool to room temperature.
  3. While rice is cooking, peel and cut cucumber and avocadoes.
  4. Once rice is cooled, add rice, nori, cucumber, crab meat, and avocado to bowl.
  5. Top with spicy mayo and sesame seeds.
  6. Serve with soy sauce and wasabi if desired.
